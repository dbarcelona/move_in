🏠 Floor Plan Creator v2

Draw your apartment with doors, windows, and furniture blocking

Grid: 20px

How to Use:

  • Draw Room: Click and drag to create room (auto-snaps to grid)
  • Doors: Click door mode, then click on room edge to add door (36" wide)
  • Windows: Click window mode, then click on room edge to add window
  • Zoom: Use 🔍+/- to change grid size (10px, 20px, 40px)
  • Furniture Blocking: Doors create no-furniture zones (realistic swing area)
  • Delete: Select item and click Delete button
