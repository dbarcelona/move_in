🏠 Floor Plan Creator

Draw your apartment layout

How to Use:

  • Draw Room: Click and drag to create a room box
  • Select/Move: Click to select a room, drag to move it
  • Resize: Drag the corner handles of a selected room
  • Label: Type a name in the text box before drawing
  • Delete: Select a room and click "Delete Selected"
